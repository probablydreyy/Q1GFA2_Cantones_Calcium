Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto was unknowingly made into the host (jinchuriki) of the Nine-Tails Fox, a dangerous and powerful tailed beast that attacked the village.
Naruto has a long-standing rivalry with Sasuke Uchiha, another prominent character in the series.
Naruto is known for his cheerful, outgoing, and determined personality. He is also famous for his catchphrase, "Believe it!" (in the English dub).
Naruto's dream is to become the Hokage, the strongest ninja and leader of his village, in order to gain the recognition and respect he has always craved.
